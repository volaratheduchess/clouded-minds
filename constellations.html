The Origin of the Storm:
Welcome to Clouded Minds

In the vast, often chaotic landscape of AI roleplay, it is easy to get lost in the noise. Clouded Minds was born from a desire to find clarity within that mist—to create a sanctuary where the art of character building is treated with the depth and narrative soul it deserves.

The Foundation: A Tribute to Norisor

The seeds of this community were sown by our founder, Norisor. It was her vision that first brought together a collective of creators who believed that a bot is more than just code and a prompt; her work serves as the foundation upon which we all continue to grow, even in her retirement.

Norisor's influence remains the steady heartbeat of our server. Their dedication to quality, world-building, and creative integrity continues to be the ongoing inspiration for our Council and our creators alike. We aren't just building bots here; we are tending to the legacy of a "Storm-Brewing" culture that Norisor pioneered.

Who We Are

Today, managed by Taekhesis (Tae) and the ArchTempest moderation team, Clouded Minds has evolved into an invite-only haven for elite bot builders. We are a community of creators specializing in platforms like Crushon.ai, Craveu.ai, LinkinLove, Saucepan.ai, and Tipsychat.

We are not a high-speed advertising hub—we're a curated space where creators and the rest of the community "steep" their ideas until they are ready to break as a storm.

The Philosophy of Clouded Minds:
A Sanctuary for the Soul

At the heart of Clouded Minds is a belief that the best stories are written in a space of peace and mutual respect. We aren't just a collection of bot builders; we are a community that prioritizes the human element behind the screen. Our philosophy is defined by these core values:

  • Support Without Borders
    We believe in lifting each other up at every stage of the creative journey. Whether you are a veteran architect of lore or a new builder finding your voice, we are here to support your growth.
  • A Space Without Judgment
    We celebrate the diversity of imagination. We understand that everyone's viewpoints, preferences, and narrative styles vary, and we provide a safe harbor to explore those ideas without fear of being judged.
  • Optimism & Uplifting Vibes
    Our atmosphere is built on positivity. We focus on what makes our craft beautiful and exciting, ensuring that every interaction within our walls leaves you feeling inspired.
  • The Mentorship Cycle
    We believe that knowledge should be shared, not hoarded. More experienced creators are encouraged to help newer ones grow, passing down the tools and wisdom needed to keep our collective standards high.
  • Calm & Drama-Free
    Clouded Minds is a sanctuary from the noise. We strictly maintain a drama-free environment to ensure the server remains a calm, beautiful place for everyone to focus on their art.

In this storm, we don't fight the wind; we learn to fly together. We are a "beating creative heart" where the only competition is with our own past work, and the goal is always to create something meaningful.
